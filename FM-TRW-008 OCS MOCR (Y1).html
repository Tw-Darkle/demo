Important Starting Instructions.
1. Ensure that the section concerned and all associated equipment is switched off, de-energised or disconnected from its power supply
2. Secure the section concerned against unintended or automatic restoration of power including any necessary measures to prevent accidental re-energising of equipment
3. Check the section concerned to be certain that the power is de-energised. Use an approved voltage- detector to verify the power status of the section.
4. Connect the section to earth. Only a section between two earthing rods is electrically safe
5. Cover adjacent parts and equipment that are still energised, against touch voltage and display warning notices.
